About Us

Founded in 1941, Coach stands for true American style and luxury. We are defined by our signature details, quality leather and classic designs that stand the test of time.

Our mission is to create objects of desire that are built to last. We believe in style that is timeless, yet always modern. From our iconic handbags to ready-to-wear collections, every piece is crafted with care and attention to detail.

Today, Coach continues to evolve, blending our rich heritage with contemporary fashion to bring you the very best in accessories and lifestyle.
